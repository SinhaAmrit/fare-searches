Cancellation & Refund Policy


Cancellation and Amendment Policy


Any changes or cancellations you wish to make to your reservation will be subject to the terms and conditions of the respective supplier. Certain tickets may be non-refundable or non-changeable. In some cases, modifications or cancellations might be allowed, but this could incur a penalty charge.

It's important to note that most airline tickets cannot be transferred to another person, and name changes are generally not permitted. Therefore, when making a reservation for scheduled flights online, ensure that the passenger names match their passports. The cancellation and amendment rules for the purchased fare are presented during the booking process; please review them carefully.

As intermediaries for airlines and consolidators, we are bound by their terms and conditions, including pricing rules and supplier conditions. When changes or refunds are possible, our website will apply administrative fees (based on our current rates) in addition to any charges imposed by the supplier. These fees cover the costs associated with managing cancellations and modifications.

Requests to modify or cancel a booking must be submitted in writing through the website and will only be processed upon receipt. Requests received outside of our operating hours will be addressed on the next operational day, and if communication with the travel provider is necessary, it will be initiated once contact is established.


Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel a booking after ticket issuance, there will be a cancellation fee per passenger, in addition to the airline fare.

Prior to proceeding with the cancellation, we advise you to contact us via phone or email to determine if your ticket allows for any refunds. If you decide to cancel, please inform us in writing. (Failure to notify us of the cancellation before the scheduled departure time or failing to check-in may result in a 'no show' status, leading to the forfeiture of all paid amounts).

If a refund is applicable, it may take around 10-16 weeks for the airline to process it. Once approved, the refund will be credited back to the original debit or credit card used for the reservation. Please inform us if this card is no longer valid.

Refunds will be net of any airline or tour operator cancellation fees. Some airlines also impose an additional fee for processing refunds. Standard website administration charges will also apply to any refund. Reservation fees, credit card charges, postage fees, and fees from previous modifications are non-refundable.

In cases of non-refundable packaged tickets, there might be an opportunity to reclaim unused taxes. Please note that not all taxes are refundable, and some airlines charge for handling such requests. These charges could exceed the refundable amount. Feel free to inquire for details when canceling your reservation.


Amendment Policy

If you want to make changes to your reservation, please contact us via phone or email. We can guide you on whether your ticket allows for alterations. If changes are possible, we may request written confirmation of your intention to modify the reservation.

All changes are subject to availability and the terms of the relevant travel provider.

If the amendment increases the total reservation cost, you will be responsible for covering the additional charges.

Any amendments will also incur the website's standard administrative fees.


Cancellations/Amendments by the Travel Supplier

Airlines retain the right to make schedule changes or, in rare cases, cancel flights due to operational reasons. While the website is not responsible for these changes, we will do our best to assist you in such situations.

If your air travel is canceled by the airline or tour operator, your rights and options will be determined by the merchant's or airline's conditions of carriage. You might be eligible for:

(a) Carriage on an alternate flight with the same airline at no extra cost;

(b) Re-routing to your destination with another carrier without additional charges;

(c) Additional rights or remedies as specified.

In case of schedule changes before your journey begins, it may not always be necessary to reissue or revalidate your tickets. However, we will advise you if such action is required. This website assumes no responsibility for flight delays en route.
